Hi, I'm Natalia Sierra. I'm 20 years old and currently studying programming. When I started, I had no idea what I was getting into since I had no prior knowledge, and fear overwhelmed me. However, I have been learning gradually, and I've started to like it. I've decided that I want to focus on frontend development.


I am currently enrolled in the Software Analysis and Development program at SENA, which lasts two years and provides training in programming, databases, systems analysis, and the development of web and mobile applications. Throughout the course, I have gained competencies in various technologies and learned to use web technologies and databases. The program includes business internships, allowing me to apply my knowledge in real-world environments and preparing me to obtain the title of Technologist in Software Analysis and Development.
